The Show

A-Town Showdown is a national intercollegiate Garba/Raas and Bollywood Fusion dance competition. The show will be held at the Robert Ferst Center for the Arts on February 13th, 2016 in Atlanta, GA. 8 Garba/Raas and 8 Bollywood Fusion teams will be selected to compete for first place in their respective category. This marks our 5th Anniversary.
